BiLiNGUAL NATURE ACADEMY LLC

Welcome to
Bilingual Nature Academy LLC

"Where Language and Nature Blossom Together."

At Bilingual Nature Academy, we provide a unique early learning experience that nurtures the brain, body, and soul through a balanced blend of child-led exploration and thoughtful classroom learning. Located on beautiful Whidbey Island, our bilingual program fosters curiosity, creativity, and confidence in both English and Spanish. What makes us unique? We embrace a holistic, child-led approach—encouraging children to follow their interests and develop a lifelong love for learning. 60% of our learning takes place outdoors, where children engage with nature, move their bodies, and explore the world around them. The other 40% takes place indoors in a more structured, carefully designed Montessori and Reggio Emilia-inspired classroom, where hands-on discovery and independence thrive. We view our environment as "The 3rd Teacher," shaping learning experiences that inspire curiosity, problem-solving, and meaningful connections. Give your child the gift of language, adventure, and discovery. Join our community and experience education that nurtures the whole child—naturally inspiring and truly enriching!

Admissions

To be considered for admission, children must be between the ages of 3 to 5 years old and be independent in self-care and toileting. Families are encouraged to browse our website and social media to learn more about our academy.

The Interview

The application process starts with a family tour of the preschool and a meeting with the Director.

Application Process

The Academy will provide a registration packet to accepted families to complete and return with the registration fee. We process applications and approve them on a first-come, first-serve basis. Returning families receive priority during the registration window. Following the enrollment process, the student is placed on our One-week trial period. Upon a successful trial period, the student will be permitted to attend our Academy until they turn 6 years old.

Faculty

Our faculty embodies the heart of a close-knit community, bringing warmth, dedication, and passion into every classroom. Committed to personalized education, our educators build strong, meaningful connections with students, adapting their teaching to nurture each child's unique potential. With a holistic approach, we foster the growth of the brain, body, and soul—ensuring that every child is not only academically enriched but also emotionally supported and physically engaged. In our learning environment, every student is known, valued, and uplifted, creating a space that feels like family.
