Admissions

To be considered for admission, children must be between the ages of 3 to 5 years old and be independent in self-care and toileting. Families are encouraged to browse our website and social media to learn more about our academy.

The Interview

The application process starts with a family tour of the preschool and a meeting with the Director.

Required Trials

Because of the uniqueness of our program we ask prospective families to begin our trial period to ensure the student, family and our staff are a good fit together. We strive to create a well-mixed and diverse class with a low teacher-student ratio.

During this time, we love to observe the child's transition into the space, engagement with other students, and response to teachers. Once accepted into our Academy the Director will extend an official offer into our Academy by email within 48 hours.

We give priority enrollment to returning students and their siblings

We will confirm all appointments via email

We do not honor improper and/or unscheduled visits at the academy

Application Process

The Academy will provide a registration packet to accepted families to complete and return with the registration fee. We process applications and approve them on a first-come, first-serve basis.
